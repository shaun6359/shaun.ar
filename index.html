Projects

MediScan Pro

Project Details

I developed an AI-driven diagnostic system leveraging ResNet-50 for the classification of pneumonia from X-ray scans, achieving over 80% accuracy on test data. This model not only provides accurate predictions but also generates automated, professional-grade medical reports to streamline diagnostic processes. To make this tool accessible to healthcare professionals, I built a full-stack web application using Flask, complete with secure user authentication to safeguard patient data. The application enables users to upload X-ray images, receive real-time diagnostic feedback, and easily generate downloadable reports in PDF format. By integrating HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I ensured an intuitive, responsive interface that delivers a seamless user experience from image upload to report generation, helping bring AI-driven insights into everyday medical workflows.

Sentivest AI

Project Details

I developed the Sentivest AI Trading Bot, an algorithmic trading system that makes automated decisions using Python, the Alpaca API, and sentiment analysis. I integrated the FinBERT machine learning model to assess the sentiment of news articles, driving buy and sell decisions with a remarkable 99.9% confidence threshold. To minimize risk and optimize returns, I designed bracket orders that included automatic take-profit and stop-loss settings. Additionally, I backtested the trading strategy using three years of historical data on the SPY (S&P 500 ETF) from Yahoo Finance, which demonstrated a consistent pattern of profitable trades.

LexiLink

Project Details

I developed a Python-based semantic similarity predictor that utilizes cosine similarity to measure the likeness between words and sentences. The core of the project involves constructing semantic descriptors from text files, capturing the contextual relationships between words based on their co-occurrence within sentences. By comparing vectors representing words, the model computes their similarity and identifies potential synonyms. This tool, with an accuracy rate of 80%, is capable of enhancing AI-generated text by suggesting meaningful and contextually relevant word substitutions, significantly improving the personalization and quality of automated writing. The model’s efficiency is driven by advanced vectorized calculations and the flexibility to process large text datasets.
